Figure 8. Kobe plots and landings of Celtic Seas stocks by trophic guild (all stocks, pelagic, flatfish, demersal). The left Kobe plots show relative fishing pressure (F/FMSY) and biomass (SSB/ MSY Btrigger) for stocks that have SSB and F relative to reference points (MSY where available, otherwise PA or Qualitative). The size of each bubble corresponds to the ICES official landings in 2014. Stocks in green are exploited below FMSY and have an SSB that is above MSY Btrigger. Stocks in red are exploited above FMSY and have an SSB that is below MSY Btrigger, exploited above FMSY and have an SSB that is above MSY Btrigger, or and vice versa. Stocks in gray are of unknown status in relation to reference points. Full stock names by stock codes are available in Annex Table A.
